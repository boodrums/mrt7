Audio Settings

Pitch range: 50Hz (low) to 1500Hz (high). Timbre selection changes the oscillator waveform.

Metronome Instructions

The MRT7 Rhythm Trainer is a visual and audible metronome designed to help practice rhythmic accuracy and timing, especially for tricky silent sections.

Keyboard Controls

  • Spacebar: Start or Stop the metronome.
  • Right Arrow (→): Increase Tempo by 1 BPM.
  • Left Arrow (←): Decrease Tempo by 1 BPM.
  • Up Arrow (↑): Increase Tempo by 5 BPM.
  • Down Arrow (↓): Decrease Tempo by 5 BPM.

Rhythm Grid & States

Click on any square in the rhythm grid to cycle its state. The grid determines the subdivision and accent pattern. The colors are:

  • Primary: Strongest accent/pitch. Used for downbeats or strong pulses.
  • Secondary: Medium accent/pitch.
  • Tertiary: Weakest accent/pitch.
  • Off: Silent step.

Bar Cycle (Play/Silent Bars)

The cycle feature allows you to practice against silent bars, forcing you to maintain tempo internally. You set the number of **Play Bars** (rhythm is audible) and **Silent Bars** (rhythm is muted).

  • Example: If set to **Play 1 Bar** and **Silent 2 Bars**, the metronome will play the pattern for 1 bar, then remain silent for 2 bars, repeating the cycle.

Count-In

Click the Count-In button to toggle between 0, 1, or 2 bars of simple audible counting before the main pattern begins.
